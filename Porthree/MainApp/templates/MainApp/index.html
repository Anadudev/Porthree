About Porthree

Porthree is a personal portfolio management platform designed to empower individuals to showcase their skills, achievements, and experiences in a captivating and professional manner. Built by aspiring software engineers at Holberton School, Porthree aims to simplify the process of creating and maintaining a portfolio, freeing users to focus on what truly matters: their career growth and personal development.
